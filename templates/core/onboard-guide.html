Setup Guide

Getting Started with MAEDIX

Follow these steps to connect your Instagram account and start automating your lead capture. Complete setup takes about 10 minutes.

‚è±Ô∏è Estimated time: 10 minutes
1

Create Your Account

Sign up or log in to access your MAEDIX dashboard

Start by creating your MAEDIX account. If you already have an account, simply log in to continue.

1
Go to the Sign Up page or click Login if you already have an account
2
Enter your email address and create a secure password
3
Verify your email address through the confirmation link sent to your inbox
2

Create Your Company Profile

Set up your real estate company in the dashboard

Once logged in, you'll need to create a company profile. This represents your real estate business or agency within MAEDIX.

1
From your dashboard, click "Create New Company" or "Add Company"
2
Enter your company name, contact information, and basic details
3
Click "Save" to create your company profile
üí° Tip

You can manage multiple companies under one MAEDIX account if you operate multiple agencies or branches.

3

Connect Your Instagram Account

Link your Instagram Business or Creator account to MAEDIX

Before connecting Instagram, you need to ensure your account meets the requirements.

‚ö†Ô∏è Prerequisites - Important
  • Instagram Professional Account: You must have an Instagram Business or Creator account (not a personal account)
  • Facebook Page: Your Instagram account must be connected to a Facebook Page
  • Facebook Page Admin: You need admin access to the Facebook Page
‚ÑπÔ∏è Don't Have a Professional Account?

You can convert your personal Instagram account to a Business or Creator account for free. Go to Instagram Settings ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Switch to Professional Account.

Once your prerequisites are met, follow these steps:

1
Go to your company profile and click "Connect Instagram"
2
Click "Connect Facebook" first - you'll be redirected to Facebook login
3
Select your Facebook Page from the list (this must be the page connected to your Instagram account)
4
Grant the necessary permissions when prompted (read messages, manage comments, etc.)
5
After Facebook connection is complete, click "Connect Instagram"
6
Enter your Instagram credentials and authorize MAEDIX to access your account
‚úì Success

Once connected, MAEDIX will begin monitoring your Instagram DMs and comments immediately.

4

Configure Default Responses

Set up your automated reply messages

Before you subscribe to AI-powered plans, you need to set up default response messages. These will be used for basic automation.

1
Navigate to "View Company" or your company settings page
2
Find the "Default Messages" section
3
Fill in the following required fields:
Default Reply Text (DMs)
"Thank you for your interest! We've received your message and one of our agents will respond shortly. In the meantime, you can browse our available properties at [your website]."
Comment Reply
"Thanks for asking! We'll send you details via DM. Please check your messages."
First Follow-up Message
"Hi! Following up on your inquiry about our property. Are you available for a quick chat to discuss your requirements? What's your preferred budget range?"
üí° Why These Messages?

Default messages ensure you never miss responding to an inquiry, even without an active AI subscription. Once you subscribe to an AI plan, intelligent responses will replace these default messages.

5

Add Your Property Listings

Create listings that AI will reference in responses

Add your active property listings to MAEDIX. These serve as the knowledge base for AI-powered responses.

1
Go to the "Listings" tab in your company dashboard
2
Click "Create New Listing" or "Add Listing"
3
Fill in property details: address, price, bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage, amenities, description
4
Upload property photos (optional but recommended)
5
If you have an Instagram post about this property, you can link the Instagram post to the listing
6
Click "Save Listing"
üí° Connecting Instagram Posts

When you link an Instagram post to a listing, MAEDIX can automatically respond to comments on that specific post with accurate property information. This creates a seamless experience for potential buyers.

6

Choose Your Subscription Plan

Select the plan that fits your business needs

Finally, subscribe to a plan to unlock AI-powered responses and lead pre-qualification.

1
Go to "View Company" section in your dashboard
2
Find the "Subscription Plans" or "Upgrade" section
3
Review available plans and select the one that suits your lead volume and team size
4
Complete the payment process to activate your subscription
‚úì You're All Set!

With an active subscription, MAEDIX will now respond to Instagram inquiries with intelligent, contextual messages, pre-qualify leads, and automatically route hot prospects to your agents.

‚ÑπÔ∏è Free Trial Available

Most plans come with a 14-day free trial. Test the AI responses and lead qualification before committing to a paid plan.
